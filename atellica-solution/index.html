How It Works

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Atellica Solution.

Atellica Solution supports over 300 assays across clinical chemistry and immunoassay disciplines, including routine chemistry panels, cardiac markers, thyroid function tests, infectious disease screening, therapeutic drug monitoring, and specialty endocrinology assays.
Atellica Solution uses a modular design where analyzer modules can be added or reconfigured based on your laboratory's evolving needs. Start with one chemistry and one immunoassay module, then scale up by adding more modules as your test volume grows — all without downtime.
A standard Atellica Solution installation takes 4–6 weeks, including site preparation, hardware setup, software configuration, LIS integration, validation testing, and staff training. Our project management team works closely with your laboratory to minimize disruption.
Atellica Solution provides industry-standard HL7 and ASTM interfaces for seamless integration with virtually any Laboratory Information System (LIS) or Hospital Information System (HIS). Our dedicated integration team ensures bidirectional communication for orders, results, and quality data.
We offer 24/7 technical support, preventive maintenance programs, remote diagnostics via our Smart Remote Services, on-site field service engineers, and comprehensive training programs. Enterprise plans include dedicated account managers and guaranteed SLA response times.
