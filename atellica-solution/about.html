Our Story

Pioneering Diagnostics for Over 125 Years

Since our founding, Siemens Healthineers has been at the forefront of medical innovation. From the earliest X-ray tubes to today's most advanced laboratory systems, we have continually pushed the boundaries of what's possible in clinical diagnostics.

The Atellica Solution platform represents the next evolution in laboratory medicine — a fully integrated system that combines clinical chemistry and immunoassay testing into a single, intelligent workflow. Our commitment is simple: help labs deliver faster, more accurate results so clinicians can make better decisions for their patients.

  • Integrated clinical chemistry & immunoassay testing
  • AI-driven workflow optimization
  • Trusted by labs in over 70 countries
  • Committed to reducing diagnostic errors
