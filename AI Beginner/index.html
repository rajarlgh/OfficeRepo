Approach

The AI Iteration Loop

  1. Define Goal: Identify the specific UX or content problem.
  2. Draft Prompt: Engineer the initial AI query.
  3. AI Output: Generate the baseline code, text, or structure.
  4. Human Refinement: Critique, adjust, and re-prompt.
  5. Feature Implementation: Integrate into the final Atellica experience.

Precision Diagnostics. Accelerated.

Atellica™ Solution integrates clinical chemistry and immunoassay testing into one scalable, automated platform — delivering faster results with uncompromising accuracy.

Integrated Chemistry & Immunoassay

Run clinical chemistry and immunoassay tests on a single consolidated platform, eliminating duplicate sample handling and reducing turnaround times by up to 40%.

Intelligent Sample Routing

Automated bi-directional sample management intelligently routes specimens to the right analyzer module, optimizing throughput and minimizing idle time.

Scalable Architecture

Flexible configurations that grow with your lab — from single-module setups to enterprise-scale multi-analyzer deployments.

Real-Time Analytics Dashboard

Monitor laboratory operations with live dashboards displaying throughput metrics, QC status, reagent levels, and predictive maintenance alerts.

Advanced Quality Assurance

Comprehensive testing frameworks with automated QC validation, multi-rule Westgard analysis, and regulatory compliance tracking.
