Slide 8

Method

The AI Iteration Loop

  1. Define Goal: Identify the specific UX or content problem.
  2. Draft Prompt: Engineer the initial AI query.
  3. AI Output: Generate the baseline code, text, or structure.
  4. Human Refinement: Critique, adjust, and re-prompt.
  5. Feature Implementation: Integrate into the final Atellica experience.
Judge's lens: Document your prompt engineering process and show how iteration moved the solution from mediocre to highly tailored.
